Team66 CCC Project

This project is about people's Positive/Negative emotions when they talk about Covid19 and Crime.

Scenario 1:
The GEO maps are about where do people like to talk about Covid19 and Crime.
Scenario 2:
The keywords that people tend to mention about Covid19 and Crime.
Scenario 3:
Explore the relationship between the number of Twitter users' friends and positive/negative emotions.
Scenario 4:
Compare 7 cities' income, employed people and positive VS negative.
Scenario 5:
See the proportion of different languages in Pos/Neg Covid/Crime Tweets.
Scenario 6:
Explore the relationship between the number of mental health people and negative emotions.
